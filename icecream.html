Mitchell's Ice Cream

Address: 688 San Jose Avenue, San Francisco, CA 94110

Telophone: (415) 648-2300

Micthell's Ice Cream is a local ice cream shop located in Bernal Heights of San Francisco. It offers a variety of treats containing ice cream including milkshakes, cakes, smoothies, sundaes, ice cream sandwiches and much more! In fact they are even voted a neighborhood favorite! They are dietary friendly meaning most of their ice creams are gluten free and do not contain any eggs. It has a wide variety of over 40 flavors so you would definitely find somehing you will like!
