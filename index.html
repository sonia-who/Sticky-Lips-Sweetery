WHY SHOULD YOU SUPPORT LOCAL BUSINESSES?

Local businesses have been the most affected shops around the world because of the pandemic and it has been challenging for them to reopen. Specifically, in the Bay area rent prices are high and many can not afford to keep reopening. Many local shops have had to close due to this and have not had a lot of support from the government to relieve this worry. They have also not had a lot of business because of people being scared to go out and they have more things to pay off for like hand sanitizer, masks, and gloves. This had lead to many financial struggles for the business owners and many neighborhood favorites had to close down. Although, if you visit their shop then you will be supporting the people behind that shop and getting a delicious treat in return!
